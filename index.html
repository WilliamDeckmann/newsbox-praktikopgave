Welcome to the Newsbox!

Here you can find articles directly from NYT.

Save your favorites

You can add an article item to your archive
by swiping and clicking the "archive button".

Open the archive

To access any archived articles,
click on the little archive icon in
the top left corner and it will take
you to your archive.

Change the settings

To change the setting, click the little
gear icon in the top right corner and
it will take your to the settings page.
Here you cna also disable certain categories
and change the theme to "dark-mode".

All good to go!

You are now ready and all good to go!
Enjoy this application and the many articles
generously provided by New York Times.
